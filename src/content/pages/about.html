About ##COMPANY NAME##

Hi there, I'm Arie and I'm the founder of ##COMPANY NAME##, I am an engineer that enjoys surfing, sports, and good food. I also love designing websites and webapps as a hobby. The first website I built was a wordpress site documenting the intersection of my two passions, surfing and engineering. Unfortunately I learned how inefficient page builders like wordpress were and using shared hosting serices such as go-daddy.

In 2024 my Dad needed a website for his small consulting business, but up front costs were quite high from local agencies. I decided to delve back into building websites to build something clean and simple for his needs. Through this I learned more about efficiently coding websites such that they loaded faster and got recognised easier by google.

In 2025 I decided to turn this hobby into a side project helping out other small businesses in Perth gain digital presence online. So now here we are. All of the websites I build are entirely functional HTML/CSS/JS code optimised for mobile first performance that allow sites to rank better.

If you're interested in getting your business online with a low cost upfront fee, drop me a line!
